About Us

Punyela Solutions is a student-run business that provides fresh, organic vegetables and fruits from university farms. We believe in healthy eating, sustainability, and supporting local student farmers.

Our mission is to make fresh produce accessible to the campus community while empowering students through entrepreneurship.
